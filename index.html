The Business and Legacy of Michael Jordan

Michael Jordan is known as one of the greatest basketball players ever, but what really stands out is how he turned his career into long-term financial success. As a senior planning for college, I chose him because he shows how success can go beyond just a job and turn into real wealth.

About

I chose Michael Jordan because I’m interested in how athletes make money outside of sports. He was born in 1963 and became famous playing for the Chicago Bulls. He won 6 championships and later became a successful businessman.

Research

  • 6× NBA Champion
  • 5× MVP
  • Career average: 30.1 points per game
  • Air Jordan brand earns billions

Jordan made more money from endorsements than basketball. His partnership with Nike created one of the most successful brands ever.
